What to review

Cash App rows can still need context.

USD values

Rows with missing or zero USD spot price should be reviewed before relying on reports.

Transfers

Bitcoin withdrawals may be owner transfers, disposals, or records that need source-backed notes.

Holdings

Declared current BTC holdings help identify missing historical records or unresolved activity.

CPA packet

Draft packets keep warnings and review decisions visible for handoff.

Need a parser fix?

Cash App exports can change over time.

If Gainz cannot identify the columns, open a parser request with sanitized headers and synthetic rows. Do not upload your real export to GitHub.
