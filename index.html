Positioning

Built for messy records, privacy, and CPA handoff.

Gainz is not trying to replace hosted crypto tax platforms for fast account sync or one-click filing. Its lane is offline reconciliation for people who need to explain old CSVs, missing basis, current holdings, prior-year evidence, and open review work without first uploading raw tax history.

Cloud apps

Fast sync and filing

Useful when you want hosted workflows, exchange connections, and direct filing paths.

Open-source tools

Private but technical

Useful for technical users, but often less focused on guided review queues and CPA packet handoff.

Gainz

Draft until resolved

Privacy and control

Offline and private by design.

Gainz runs on your computer. It does not require a hosted account, exchange API sync, wallet sync, or transaction-history upload for the reconciliation workflow. Tax evidence scans default to reference-only records unless you explicitly choose packet copy.

  • No required transaction-history upload
  • Desktop workflow for Windows and macOS
  • Reference-only tax evidence by default
  • Audit packets include privacy and CPA handoff notes
Fit

Who Gainz is for.

Good fit Historical CSVs, missing basis, current holdings gaps, evidence inventory, privacy-sensitive review, and CPA handoff. Not the goal One-click exchange sync, hosted filing, tax advice, or replacing a qualified professional review.
