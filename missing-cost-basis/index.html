Cost basis reconstruction

Missing crypto cost basis does not have to stay vague.

Gainz helps turn "basis missing" into reviewable questions: which sale needs earlier acquisition lots, which source file may be missing, and which transfer needs user research.

Download Gainz Read basis method

Good fit

Old exchange exports, transferred coins, incomplete CSVs, and sale rows that do not have enough earlier acquisition quantity.

What Gainz shows

Specific missing-record questions.

Missing acquisition basis

Shows the asset, date, sale quantity, and how much earlier basis is still needed.

Transfer ambiguity

Separates owner transfers from sends that may need to be reviewed as disposals.

Review decisions

Lets users mark items resolved, needs research, ignored for draft, or sent to a CPA.

Packet evidence

Keeps unresolved items visible in the draft packet and reconciliation work order.

Workflow

How to approach missing basis.

  1. Import every exchange CSV and wallet/export file you can locate.
  2. Declare current holdings so Gainz can compare imported activity with what you actually hold.
  3. Run FIFO basis linking only after import warnings and obvious holdings gaps are reviewed.
  4. Use the Guided Review Queue to work missing-basis items one at a time.
  5. Generate a draft packet that shows what is resolved and what still needs research.

Not tax advice

Gainz organizes records and review work. It does not decide tax treatment for you. Review outputs with a qualified tax professional before filing.
