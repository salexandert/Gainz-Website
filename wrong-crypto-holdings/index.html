Common causes

Holdings gaps usually point to missing context.

Missing source files

An old exchange, broker, or wallet export may be absent from the import set.

Owner transfers

Sends and receives may represent movement between your own accounts rather than sales.

Disposals or losses

Some sends may need research as disposals, trades, lost assets, fees, or other documented events.

Duplicate history

Full-history exports can overlap with yearly exports and inflate activity if imported twice.

Gainz approach

Make the discrepancy explainable.

The goal is not to hide a mismatch. The goal is to identify what records, user decisions, or professional review are needed before relying on generated reports.
