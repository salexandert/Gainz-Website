Packet contents

Built for review, not mystery exports.

Status first

README_FIRST.md and PACKET_STATUS.md explain draft status and blockers.

CPA handoff

FOR_CPAS.md and CPA_HANDOFF.md summarize assumptions, questions, and review needs.

Evidence manifest

Shows generated files, copied sources, reference-only evidence, hashes, and missing paths.

Work order

Lists unresolved blockers by priority so the next research step is clear.

Unknown gap memos

Documents what remains unknown, what evidence to look for, user notes, and CPA questions without forcing certainty.

Privacy

Reference-only evidence by default.

Tax evidence scans can list file references without copying tax documents into the packet. Files are copied only when the user explicitly selects packet copy.
