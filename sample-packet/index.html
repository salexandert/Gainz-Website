Synthetic demo output

Inspect a Gainz audit packet.

This sample ZIP is generated from demo CSVs only. It shows the packet structure, status files, CPA notes, evidence manifest, work order, and Form 8949-style totals before you use Gainz with real data.

Inside the ZIP

What the sample demonstrates.

README_FIRST.md

Human orientation, folder map, sharing reminder, and where to start.

PACKET_STATUS.md

Readiness, blockers, warnings, evidence counts, and work order review counts.

FOR_CPAS.md

CPA-facing review order and professional-judgment prompts.

CPA_HANDOFF.md

How Gainz generated the packet and what source material should be checked.

PRIVACY_AND_EVIDENCE_HANDLING.md

Reference-only evidence, copied evidence, local storage, and sharing cautions.

03_manifests/evidence_manifest.csv

Copied source files, reference-only evidence records, missing paths, sizes, and hashes.

01_reports/reconciliation_work_order.csv

Itemized review queue with priority, issue, recommended action, and review decision columns.

01_reports/form_8949_totals.csv

Short-term, long-term, and total proceeds, basis, and gain/loss from linked lots.

Folder tree

How the packet is organized.

The sample packet keeps orientation notes at the root, generated reports in one folder, copied source files in another, and manifests in a separate verification folder.
